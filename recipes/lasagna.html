Ingredients

  • Lasagna pasta, 28 pieces to be exact
  • 300 grams of minced ground beef
  • One red paprika
  • Bechamel
  • Vegetables of your choice (y'know the ones that go in lasagna)

Steps

  1. Saute your garlic in olive oil
  2. Put your meat in and cook until ready
  3. Throw in the vegetables of your choice
  4. Tomato sauce goes in the pot
  5. Don't forget to season everything ya greedy fuck
  6. After thats ready, prepare your pasta
  7. First, put in a layer of your meat sauce
  8. Then, put on a layer of bechamel
  9. Finally, add in a layer of pasta
  10. Repeat until all ingredients are used
  11. Bake at 300 degrees fahrenheit for 40 minutes and then your done
